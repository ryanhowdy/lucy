User Reputation

How to Earn Points

Tickets

New Ticket (10 points)
If you create a new ticket and that ticket gets accepted, you will receive 10 points.

Resolve a Ticket (25 points)
If you change the status of a ticket that is assigned to you, to resolved, you will receive 25 points.

Handle a Ticket (5 points)
If you change any ticket from new to any other status, you will receive 5 points.

Get Assigned a Ticket (2 points)
If someone assignes a ticket to you, you will receive 2 points.

 

Comments

Upvote a Comment (1 point)
When you upvote another user comment, you will receive 1 point.

Receive an Upvote (10+ points)
when someone upvotes a comment you have made, you will receive 10 points times the level of the person who upvoted you.
Ex: If a level 5 person upvotes you, you will get 50 points.

 

Discussions

Answer is Accepted (50 points)
If you respond to a question on the support dicussion board and your answer is accepted, you will receive 50 points.

 

Suggestions

Suggestion is Accepted (25 points)
If you make a suggestion and it gets turned into a ticket, you will receive 25 points.

Your Suggestion is Upvoted (10 points)
If a suggestion you made gets upvoted by another user, you will receive 10 points.

Upvote a Suggestion (2 points)
When you upvote another user's suggestion, you will receive 2 points.

 

Translation

Translate a String (10 points)
If you translate a word or phrase into another language, you will receive 10 points.

 

Code

Commit a Line of Code (5 points)
If you commit a line of code, you will receive 5 points.

Reputation Levels

Every user starts with 0 points and in level 0. Each time you gain enough points to pass the minimum number of points for a level, you move up to that level.

To figure out the points needed for each level, you simply take the level number and put a 1 in front of that many 0's. So level 5 would be 1 fowllowed by 5 0's, or 100,000.

  • Level 0 (0 points)
  • Level 1 (10 points)
  • Level 2 (100 points)
  • Level 3 (1,000 points)
  • Level 4 (10,000 points)
  • Level 5 (100,000 points)
  • Level 6 (1,000,000 points)
  • Level 7 (10,000,000 points)
  • Level 8 (100,000,000 points)
  • Level 9 (1,000,000,000 points)
  • and so on...
